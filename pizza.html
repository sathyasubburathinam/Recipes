Easy Homemade Pizza Sauce

This pizza sauce recipe is easy to make with tomato paste, olive oil, garlic, and herbs.
No cooking is required, and it tastes delicious — just like you'd get at a pizzeria!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water, or as needed
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • ⅓ cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ tablespoon dried oregano
  • ½ tablespoon dried basil

Directions

Step1

Gather all ingredients.

Step2

Mix together water, tomato paste, and olive oil in a large bowl or jar.
Add garlic, oregano, basil, rosemary, salt, and pepper; mix well.

Step3

For best results, let sauce stand for several hours to allow the flavors to blend.
No cooking is necessary; just spread on pizza crust.
